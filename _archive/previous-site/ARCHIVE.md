# Previous site archive

This folder contains the source of the scrolling editorial Dare to Doyle website that preceded the playable 3D world.

It is retained only as a design and implementation reference. Nothing in this folder is imported by the active Vite application or included in the production bundle.

Archived material includes:

- the previous page composition and entry point;
- section-based project, social, playground, and ending views;
- the former custom cursor and progress rail;
- the previous global stylesheet;
- the original abstract React Three Fiber scene;
- the previous project README.

Generated output, dependencies, caches, and obsolete assets were intentionally not archived.
